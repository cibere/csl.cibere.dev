How do I get my discord id?

Method 1: By mentioning yourself

Step 1: Ping yourself in a discord channel
Step 2: Edit that message and add a backslash (\) infront of the ping.
Step 3: Copy the numbers in the middle, thats your id


Method 2: By turning on developer mode

Step 1: Open up your user settings
Step 2: Navigate to App Settings -> Advanced
Step 3: Enable Developer Mode
Step 4: Find yourself as the author of a message, in the member list, or in dms
Step 5: Right click on your avatar, and press Copy User ID, you id is now on your clipboard
