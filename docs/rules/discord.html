Discord Rules

1) Follow discord's TOS and Community Guidelines

We don't want to get in trouble with discord, do we?


2) No NSFW Content / Gore

This includes talking about it,
cropping content to be able to send it,
and content that is suggestive rather than NSFW.


3) Be Respectful

This includes not being toxic,
No engaging in hate speech,
No sending death threats,
No randomly pinging people,
and overrall just be nice.


4) Don't Be Annoying

This includes spamming.
This also applies to voice channels.
This also includes repeatedly pinging someone.


5) English Only

This includes codes, since this rule is here
due to our staff only speaking English,
and thus not being able to moderate in
other languages.


6) No malicious content

This includes IP grabbing websites,
and fake virus videos.


7) No Advertising

This includes in DMs.


Remember, moderators can moderate at their own descression.
If it was deemed unfair, higher staff members will deal with the situation.
