Match Rules

1) Do not argue with the referee

During the game, go with what the referee says.
If you have a complaint, create a reporting ticket.
Additionally, the staff's verdict may be a replay of the game.


2) No Wasting Time

Just don't... play and have fun


3) 15 Minutes to prepare before force-start

Each team has up to 15 minutes to prepare before a game.
Once 15 minutes is up, the game will be started by force.
We game to play... not wait.


4) You must not concistently exceed 250ms

If you concistently exceed 250ms, you are now allowed to play.
If you continue to play, you will be force-released.


5) No substitutions can happen without the referee's concent.

The referee must allow substitutions.
They are also not permitted while the ball is in play.


6) 7 players are required to play.

If your team can not field 7 players, you must forfeit the game.
If neither team can play, a tie will be the game's result.


7) The referee must submit the results

The referee must submit the results to #gametimes where the gametime was originally posted.


8) All players must have a position.

If we wanted to watch a big blob, we would go watch some 7 year olds.
Please make sure all players have position, and are playing it. (to an extent)


9) GK Must stay on line during PK's.

In the event of a penaltie kick, the goal keeper must stay on the line until the ball is hit.


10) 2 yellow cards across two games is punishable

If you receive 2 yellow cards across 2 different games, you are not safe.
You will not be allowed to participate in your team's next two games.


11) If the GK has the ball, everyone must get out of the box.

If the goal keeper has the ball, everyone must get out of their box to give them space.


12) All players must be signed to their team.

In order to play, you must be signed to the team in the discord server.
This includes being verified through bloxlink in the discord server.



Remember, moderators can moderate at their own descression.
If it was deemed unfair, higher staff members will deal with the situation.
